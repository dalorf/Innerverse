FREQUENTLY ASKED

How do online sessions work?

+

Our virtual sessions are conducted via Zoom or your preferred video platform. You'll receive a private link and detailed preparation guide 24 hours before your session. All you need is a quiet space and stable internet connection.

Are the sessions really free?

+

Yes! We're offering completely free sessions during our launch period to build our community and gather testimonials. No hidden fees, no credit card required. This is our gift to you.

What if I miss a scheduled session?

+

Life happens! Just give us 24 hours notice and we'll reschedule at no cost. We offer flexible timing to accommodate your schedule, including evening and weekend slots.

What should I expect from my first session?

+

Your first session focuses on understanding your current situation, identifying mental blocks, and creating a clear vision for your path forward. You'll leave with actionable insights and a personalized clarity plan.
